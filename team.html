The dream team

We are a dedicated group of individuals who share a common commitment to making a difference in the lives of those in need. We are a small yet passionate team of both men and women, united by our Christian faith, who have volunteered our time and resources to serve the community. With the invaluable support of international partners, we venture into the community to identify families in dire need. Our mission is to extend a helping hand, preparing essential food bags to alleviate their immediate challenges. But our assistance goes beyond financial, health, and material aid; we offer words of encouragement and guidance when needed, ensuring that families feel supported in their journey towards a better life. We work closely with churches, collaborating with them to identify families in need within their congregations. These churches not only provide crucial support in identifying those who require assistance. In partnership with pastors, preachers of the word, and other dedicated men and women of faith, we create a unique and impactful experience. On the day we distribute food to the 100 families, these individuals join us to witness, preach, or share a sermonette. Through these powerful messages of hope and faith, we have witnessed remarkable transformations. Some individuals have found solace and purpose, accepting Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior. We are more than just a team; we are a family that believes in the power of compassion and community. As we strive to make a positive impact, we are always looking to expand our circle.
